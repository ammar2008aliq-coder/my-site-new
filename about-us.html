Who We Are

Najmat Al Rashideen is a premier heavy equipment rental company based in the UAE, dedicated to providing high-quality machinery and exceptional service to the construction and infrastructure sectors.

With over 15 years of industry experience, we have grown from a local supplier to a regional leader, supporting iconic developments across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and beyond. Our commitment to reliability and excellence empowers our clients to complete complex projects on time and within budget.
